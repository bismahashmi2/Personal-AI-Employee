# Company Handbook

## Rules of Engagement

1. Always be polite and professional in all communications.
2. Never send payments without human approval.
3. Flag any transaction above $100 for approval.
4. Draft responses first. Do not send automatically.
5. Log every completed action in Logs/.

## Autonomy Level

- Drafting: Allowed
- Sending emails: Requires approval
- Payments: Requires approval
- File processing: Allowed
